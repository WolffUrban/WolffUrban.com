With the Hotel Equity Fund, Maritz, Wolff has acquired 18 luxury urban and resort hotels in the United States, Canada, Asia-Pacific and the Caribbean, with a current total of 6,000 hotel rooms generating in excess of $700 million in annual revenues. The portfolio of luxury hotels and resorts has a combined asset value in excess of $1.5 billion and is comprised of properties operated by Four Seasons, Ritz-Carlton, Fairmont, and Rosewood. Through active oversight and asset management, Maritz, Wolff has maximized cash flow while maintaining and enhancing long-term asset value. Improved bottom-lines at Maritz, Wolff owned assets have translated into attractive annual returns on investment (via cash distributions) for Maritz, Wolff investors, plus equity returns in excess of the firms targeted 25% IRR objective.
