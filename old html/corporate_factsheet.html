FACT SHEET

Profile

Maritz, Wolff & Co. is a hotel investment group that acquires full-service urban and resort hotel properties and works directly with the hotel management to enhance revenues and reduce costs. Founded in 1994 with the purchase of The Ritz-Carlton, St. Louis, Maritz, Wolff’s holdings today exceed $1.5 billion in asset value.

Hotel Equity Fund, L.P.

Hotel Equity Fund, L.P. has nearly $500 million in invested capital in its portfolio of hospitality related assets which have a combined asset value in excess of $1.5 billion. With the Hotel Equity Fund, L.P. Maritz, Wolff has acquired 18 of the finest urban and resort hotels in the United States, Canada, Asia-Pacific, and the Caribbean, with a total of 6,000 rooms which generate in excess of $700 million in annual gross revenues. Through active oversight and asset management, Maritz, Wolff maximizes cash flow while maintaining and enhancing long-term asset value.

Exit Strategy

Potential exits include portfolio sale, individual property sale, IPO via REIT, refinancing/recapitalization, institutional joint venture, etc.
Return Hotel Equity Fund seeks to maximize current cash distributions from its properties and targets a 25% internal rate on return for its investments.
